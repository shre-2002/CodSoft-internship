ABOUT US

PAWSOME PET was founded with a simple mission: to create a one-stop destination for all pet owners. Our journey began five years ago when our team realized the need for a pet shop that offers not only high-quality pet supplies but also exceptional customer service. With years of experience as pet owners ourselves, we understand the joy and responsibility that comes with having a furry companion. That's why we are committed to serving you and your pets with utmost care and dedication.

BREEDS

German Shepard

German Shepherd is a highly intelligent breed that was originally bred for herding sheep.

Labrador

The Labrador is a loyal companion, babysitter and best friend, so could well be the perfect family dog.

Siberian Husky

Huskies are friendly dogs that are typically good with kids and other pets, but they are also very smart.

Rottweiler

Active, intelligent dogs, they are fully confident enough to act on their own, so they need guidance right from the start.

Pomeranian

Pomeranians are easily trained and make fine watchdogs and perky pets for families.

Dachshund

Dachshunds tend to have independent personalities, but that doesn't mean they don't like to join in on family fun.

Dalmatian

Dalmatians are highly energetic, playful and sensitive dogs. They are loyal to their family and good with children.

Dobermann

Dobermanns can be calm and loyal dogs given the right training, but they are still very energetic and so suit active households.

Shih Tzu

The Shih Tzu is known to be especially affectionate with children. They make a great pet if you live in an apartment or lack a big backyard.
